On this site, I will go over the effect of battle music in Pokémon on players when dealing with difficult challenges. This will pertain to the evil organization, the Elite Four, and the Champion within this series.



At the beginning of this game, you get to choose between three different starters, which will play a vital role in your journey. From there, the game loop begins. You go town to town getting stronger, collecting new Pokémon, and collecting Gym Badges. The game has three objectives:
1. Collect all 8 gym badges so you can gain access to the Elite Four and beat the champion
2. Take down team Galactic
